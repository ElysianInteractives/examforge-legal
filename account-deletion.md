# Account and Data Deletion for ExamForge

**Effective date:** May 17, 2026

ExamForge is published by **North Star Labs**. This page explains how to request deletion of your ExamForge account access and associated data.

## Request Account Deletion

To request account deletion, email:

**Elysianinteractives@gmail.com**

Use the subject line:

**ExamForge account deletion request**

Please include the Google account email address you used to sign in to ExamForge so we can identify the request.

## What Is Deleted

ExamForge is designed as an offline-first app and does not operate a backend server that stores your classes, decks, flashcards, notes, recordings, review history, or backups.

When you request deletion, we will delete or anonymize any developer-controlled account records associated with your request, if any exist.

To delete data stored on your device, you can:

- Delete classes, decks, cards, materials, exams, and backups inside ExamForge where those controls are available.
- Use Android system settings to clear ExamForge app storage.
- Uninstall ExamForge from your device.

Clearing app storage or uninstalling the app removes locally stored app data from that device, subject to Android's normal storage and backup behavior.

## What May Be Kept

Some records are controlled by third parties or may need to be retained for legitimate reasons:

- Google Sign-In account information is managed through your Google account.
- Google Play purchase and order records are managed by Google Play.
- Support or deletion request emails may be retained for up to 90 days so we can complete the request and keep a record of the action taken.
- Records required for legal, tax, fraud-prevention, or security purposes may be retained as required by law or platform policy.

## Partial Data Deletion

You may also request deletion of specific data, such as support correspondence, without requesting deletion of account access. Email the same address above with a description of the data you want deleted.
